About Us

Welcome to MiniYantra, a robotics program proudly initiated by PI Innovations. Our mission is to showcase and promote groundbreaking work in Robotics, Automation, and Artificial Intelligence, providing an outstanding platform for students and tech enthusiasts across Nepal.

PI Innovations has committed itself to equipping the next generation with practical knowledge and skills that go beyond theoretical understanding. We create an environment where learners can explore, innovate, and thrive in the exciting world of technology.

At MiniYantra, we host a variety of events, including exhibitions, competitions, and workshops, all aimed at inspiring creativity and collaboration. We believe in empowering young minds to transform ideas into reality, fostering a community of tech-savvy individuals ready to shape the future.
